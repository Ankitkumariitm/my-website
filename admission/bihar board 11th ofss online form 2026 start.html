OFSS Bihar 11th Admissions Online Form 2026

Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released a notification on its official website for the Online Facilitation System for Students (OFSS) for admission to Intermediate (Class 11) 2026 in schools and colleges. The application form has started on 08 April 2026, and candidates can fill out the form until 18 April 2026. Candidates must check the complete details for the OFSS Bihar 11th Admission Online Form 2026 given below.

Eligblity

Passed Class 10 (High School) exam from BSEB, ICSE, CBSE, or any recognized state board in India.

important date

Online Apply Last Date 18 April 2026
