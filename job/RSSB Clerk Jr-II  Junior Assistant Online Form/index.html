RSSB Clerk Jr-II / Junior Assistant Recruitment 2026

Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB), has released a Notification on official website for the recruitment of Clerk Gr-II/ Junior Assistant Post. This recruitment is for 10,644 positions. RSSB Application Form will start from 15 January 2026 & the candidates can apply till the 13 February 2026. Minimum age required is 18 Years & The Maximum Age Is 40 Years as on 01 January 2027. Candidates must check the complete details for RSSB Clerk Jr-II / Junior Assistant Recruitment 2026

Eligblity

Candidates must have passed 12th (Senior Secondary) from a recognized board. They should possess any one computer qualification such as NIELIT O/CCC, COPA/DPCS, Diploma/Degree/Certificate in Computer Science or Computer Application, 12th with Computer subject, Polytechnic Diploma in Computer Science, RSCIT, or any equivalent government-recognized qualification. Candidates must also have working knowledge of Hindi in Devnagari script and knowledge of Rajasthan culture.
