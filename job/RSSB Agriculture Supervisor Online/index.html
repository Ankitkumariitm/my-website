RSSB Agriculture Supervisor Recruitment 2026

Post Date: January 14, 2026 12:19 pm Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB), has released a notification on official website for the recruitment of Agriculture Supervisor. This recruitment is for 1100 positions. RSSB Application Form has started on 13 January 2026 & the candidates can apply till the 11 February 2026. Minimum age required is 18 Years & The Maximum Age Is 40 Years as on 01 January 2027. Candidates must check the complete details for RSSB Agriculture Supervisor Recruitment 2026

Eligblity

  • Candidates must have completed their Graduation in B.Sc. (Agriculture) or B.Sc. (Agriculture-Horticulture) from a recognized university will be eligible for this recruitment. Or
  • Senior Secondary (10+2) with Agriculture under 10+2 Scheme or Old Scheme from a recognized board/institution.
  • Knowledge of working in written Hindi in Devnagari script and knowledge of Rajasthan culture.
  • All candidates must read the official information completely before applying.
