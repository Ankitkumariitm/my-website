Bihar police bpssc ASI recruitment 2026

The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) will release the official notification for the recruitment of Assistant Sub Inspector (ASI) ‚Äì Operation posts in the year 2026. This recruitment drive will be conducted under the Bihar Police Department to fill vacant positions with eligible and qualified candidates. Through this recruitment process, selected candidates will be appointed as ASI (Operation) and will be responsible for assisting in police operations, maintaining law and order, supporting field and technical operations, and performing various security-related duties. This recruitment offers an excellent opportunity for candidates who aspire to serve in the Bihar Police and contribute to public safety and administration.

Eligblity

Candidates must have passed B.Sc with Physics as a subject and secured at least 50% marks. For SC/ST candidates, the minimum required marks are 45%.
